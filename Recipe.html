Mutton Biryani

Biryani is loved by almost every person in India if the World. Be it veg biryani or chicken biryani it’s just amazing in taste. I prepared Mutton biryani which is the original biryani as said by many people. Have this biryani with Raita (Preparation Time: 5 Minutes) and thank me later.
Follow this easy step by step recipe make people around you happy with this Mutton biryani.
Preparation Time: 10 Minutes
Cooking Time: 30 Minutes

Ingredients to prepare Mutton Biryani:

  • Oil - 1 cup
  • Cinnamon - 1 small piece
  • Cardamom - 5
  • Cloves - 4
  • Onion - 5 large sliced thinly
  • Shallots / Sambar Onion - 20 grind to a paste
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - ½ cup
  • Tomato - 4 large cubed
  • Chilli Powder - 3 tblsp
  • Garam Masala Powder - 1 tblsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander Leaves - 1 cup
  • Mint Leaves - 1 cup
  • Curd - 1 cup
  • Green Chillies - 2 slit
  • Lemon Juice - 2 tblsp
  • Seeraga Samba Rice - 5 cups
  • Water - 8 cup
  • Mutton - 1 kg
  • Roast & Grind: Cinnamon - 3 stick, Cardamom - 15 , Cloves - 1 tblsp

Instructions to prepare Mutton Biryani:

  1. Take cinnamon, cardamom and cloves in a dry pan and toast it well. Cool it down and powder it fine. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil and coconut oil in a pressure cooker or a wide pan.
  3. Add in cinnamon, cloves and cardamom and let it sizzle.
  4. Add in onions, pureed shallots and mix well. Cook that till golden brown.
  5. Add in ginger garlic paste and mix well.
  6. Add in tomatoes and mix well. Cook that till mushy.
  7. Add in curd, green chillies and mix well.
  8. Add in mutton and salt and mix well.
  9. Add in all spice powder and mix well.
  10. Add in water and bring it to a boil. Cover and pressure cook for 5 to 6 whistle till mutton is done.
  11. Now add in rice, coriander leaves and mint leaves and mix well.
  12. Cook on high heat for 10 mins. Add lemon juice and mix well.
  13. Simmer the pan and cover with the lid. Dont put whistle.
  14. Simmer for 15 mins.
  15. Remove and fluff the rice.
  16. Serve.
